Safra Adibua

Founder & Culinary Director

Safra Adibua is the visionary behind Taste Cradle, where the "art of the bite" is perfected. With a relentless focus on premium presentation and authentic flavor, Safra has built a reputation for creating culinary experiences that linger long after the last crumb.

Specializing in luxury gift collections and corporate catering, her work is defined by a "no-nonsense" approach to quality. Through Safra Living, she bridges the gap between high-end pastry arts and the comfort of home-cooked excellence.
