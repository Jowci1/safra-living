Safra Adibua

Founder & Head Chef

Safra Adibua is the visionary behind Taste Cradle, where culinary precision meets soulful hospitality. With a background rooted in the art of professional pastry and gourmet event catering, she has transformed Safra Living into a sanctuary for food lovers.

Beyond the kitchen, Safra is an educator. Through her Academy, she curates the secrets of the "perfect bite," teaching aspiring chefs the discipline of plating, the science of small chops, and the business of luxury catering.
