Chiwendu Safra Adibua

Founder & Culinary Director

My journey into the culinary world was deeply inspired by my father, who was a chef. Watching his dedication and creativity in the kitchen motivated me to discover my own passion for cooking. His influence played a major role in shaping who I am today and inspiring me to follow a similar path.

Beyond simply continuing his legacy, cooking has become something very personal to me. It brings me joy, creates meaningful memories, and gives me a strong sense of purpose. The kitchen is where I feel most inspired and where I can fully express my creativity and passion.

I am driven by the desire to showcase my skills and share my culinary talent with the world. Every time I step into the kitchen, my goal is to create something special, memorable, and exceptional. For me, cooking is not just a skill — it is happiness, passion, and a way of life.
